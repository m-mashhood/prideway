Pride Way General Contracting

Pride Way General Contracting have 16 years of vast experience in the field of Structural and nonstructural metal works (Steel, Aluminum, Civil works). Our team of Engineers (Designers, Draughtsman, Production Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Civil Engineers) with very good experience, plays a vital role in executing the projects match the client requirements. The below machineries are the key assets to our company to make impossible things to possible.

We are specialized in the field of Construction & Architectural Metal and steel works Interiors and Fitouts, Infrastructure services, Landscaping, structural fabrication & erection etc. The Company was established in 2006 and is an ISO 9001:2015, 14001:2015, 45001:2018 registered in Abu Dhabi. The company has a proven track record of delivering several high profile solutions.

Pride Way render services like procurement, construction, and maintenance & innovative services to government and private sector clients in diverse industries. For more than a decade, clients have trusted us to successfully, ethically, and safely complete their capital projects.

Building and maintaining relationships with our customers is at the heart of our business and we have a planning and management style that is consultative and systematic.
